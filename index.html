About

Hi, I'm Shierly.

I am a Registered Counsellor and Psychotherapist supporting adults and young people through emotional, relational, and life challenges.

My experience across counselling, psychotherapy, and community settings shapes my commitment to creating supportive spaces where clients feel genuinely understood and empowered. Therapy here extends beyond conversation — into deeper self-understanding, relational shifts, and forward movement.

FAQ

Good things to know.

Still have a question? Send a message — happy to help.

Where are sessions held?

In person in East Melbourne, VIC, or online via Telehealth anywhere in Australia — whichever feels most comfortable for you.

Do I need a referral or a diagnosis?

No referral, diagnosis, or crisis is required. You're welcome to begin whenever you feel ready.

Who do you work with?

Adults and young people navigating emotional, relational, and life challenges — from anxiety and burnout to life transitions and questions of identity.

What happens in the first session?

We gently explore what's bringing you here, talk through what you're hoping for, and set goals together — all at your own pace.

What are your fees?

Current fees are discussed during your free intro call. As an ACA Level 2 Registered Counsellor, rebates may be available through some private health funds.

How do I get started?

Book a free 15-minute intro call using the form below, or email hello@shierlytherapy.com.au.
